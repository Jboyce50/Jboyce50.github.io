About Flying B Outfitters

Based out of central Montana, about 20 miles south east of lewistown, Flying B outfitters is a family ran outfitting business thats been around for about 25 years. We offer a vast amount of wildlife to hunt; elk, mule deer, whitetail deer, antelope, upland bird, and all species trout fishing. Hunts consist of five star meals and suitable wall tent housing. We an garentee along with a high success rate, a great time wit great people in the best hills Montana has to offer.
